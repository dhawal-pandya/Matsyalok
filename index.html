Matsyalok

मत्स्यलोक · the realm of fish

This is my small simulation of the ocean, full of matsya (fish). Have a look: they gather into bait-balls, scatter, and hunt one another, and none of it is scripted. It all falls out of a few simple rules about size and sight.

Three kinds share the water: a silvery sardine shoal grazing the red drift of krill, the quick green mackerel that work the shoal, and a brown grouper that lurks and darts. You can be any of them too. Just click a fish to take the wheel.

Click a fish to possess it · WASD / mouse to swim · Space to dart or lunge · C control scheme · F follow · P pause · Esc release.

The Data tab has live charts and dials if you'd like to tinker. Turn off Biting and Hunger and the same fish just herd the shoal instead.
